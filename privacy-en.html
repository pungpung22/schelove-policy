Privacy Policy

Schelove - K-pop Schedule Calendar

📊 Google Play Data Safety Summary

This summary maps directly to the "Data Safety" form on Google Play Console.

  • Collected: email, name, profile photo, schedule info, preferred idol, Advertising ID, IP, device info
  • Shared: schedule / preferred idol → OpenAI (for AI cheer message generation, no personal identifiers) / Advertising ID, IP → Google AdMob
  • No sale of data.
  • Encrypted in transit: HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Data deletion: in-app [Settings → Delete Account] or email pung0805@gmail.com
  • Target audience: general users aged 13+ (K-pop schedule app, not covered by the Google Play Families program)

1. Purpose of Collecting and Using Personal Information

Schelove (the "App"), operated by PungPung Studio (the "Operator"), collects and uses personal information for the following purposes:

  • Member registration and management: Identity verification and user identification for membership services
  • Service provision: Schedule registration, D-day notifications, AI speech style system
  • Service improvement: Analysis of service usage records for service enhancement
  • Notification services: Push notifications for registered schedules

2. Personal Information Collected

Required Items

  • Google account information: Email address, name, profile photo URL
  • Device information: Device identifier (for push notifications)

Information Generated During Service Use

  • Registered schedule information (event name, date, category)
  • Preferred idol/group information
  • AI speech style settings
  • D-day notification settings
  • Service usage records

Information collected by third-party SDKs

  • Google AdMob: Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA), IP address, device info, app usage events
  • OpenAI: schedule / preferred idol / speech style (transmitted for AI cheer message generation; no personal identifiers)
  • Firebase / FCM: device token (for push notifications)

Information we do NOT collect (data minimization)

  • Sensitive data: national ID, credit card, bank account, biometric data, health / religious / political data
  • Communications data: phone number, SMS, call history, contacts (address book)
  • Hardware identifiers: IMEI, MAC address, serial number (only the resettable Advertising ID is used)
  • Precise location: GPS or Wi-Fi-based precise location
  • On-device files: gallery scans, other apps' data, list of installed apps
  • Real-time inputs: microphone, camera, keystrokes, screen captures

3. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

User's personal information is, in principle, destroyed without delay when the purpose of collection and use is achieved.

  • Upon account deletion: Immediately deleted
  • Service usage records: Immediately deleted upon account deletion

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The App does not, in principle, provide users' personal information to external parties. However, the following cases are exceptions:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by investigative authorities in accordance with legal procedures

AI Service Provision

The App uses OpenAI's services to generate AI cheerful messages. The following information may be transmitted to OpenAI during this process:

  • Registered schedule information (event name, date, category)
  • Preferred idol/group information
  • AI speech style settings

Personal identifying information such as email and name is not transmitted. OpenAI's privacy policy can be found here.

Advertising Services (Google AdMob)

This app serves advertisements through Google AdMob. The following information may be transmitted to and collected by Google for personalized advertising:

  • Advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information and IP address
  • App usage history

You can limit personalized ads by resetting / deleting the Advertising ID (Android) or disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track" (iOS).

AdMob advertising partners

AdMob may mediate or bid through Google-approved third-party ad networks (e.g., Meta Audience Network, AppLovin, Unity Ads).

  • Google-certified ad buyers and supply partners
  • How Google partner sites and apps use information
  • Google Advertising Policies

5. Procedure and Method of Personal Information Destruction

User's personal information is transferred to a separate DB after the purpose is achieved and is destroyed after a certain period of storage according to internal policies and information protection reasons under relevant laws.

Destruction Method

  • Personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using technical methods that prevent record reproduction.

Users can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request to view personal information
  • Request for correction of errors
  • Request for deletion
  • Request to stop processing

These rights can be exercised through the settings menu in the App or via email (pung0805@gmail.com).

7. How to Delete Your Account (Google Play requirement)

In compliance with Google Play's Account deletion requirement, you can delete your account and data through any of these paths:

  • In-app (recommended): Settings > Delete Account → immediate deletion
  • Web: Account Deletion Guide

The following data will be permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion:

  • Member information (email, name, profile photo)
  • All registered schedules
  • Preferred idol/group information

  • D-day notification settings
  • All service usage records

Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please decide carefully.
