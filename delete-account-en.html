Account Deletion Guide

Schelove - K-pop Schedule Calendar

Please Read Before Deleting

Deleting your account will permanently remove all data and cannot be recovered.

All your schedules, D-day notifications, AI speech style settings, and other information will be deleted. Please decide carefully.

Delete from the App

1
Open the App

Launch the Schelove app.

2
Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon from the bottom menu.

3
Select Delete Account

Tap the "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the settings menu.

4
Confirm Deletion

Review the notice and tap "Delete" to immediately delete your account.

Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete from the app, you can request via email.

  • To: pung0805@gmail.com
  • Subject: [Schelove] Account Deletion Request
  • Content: Please include the email address you registered with

Your request will be processed within 3 business days after confirmation.

Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted when you delete your account:

  • Member information (email, name, profile photo)
  • All registered schedules
  • D-day notification settings
  • Preferred idol/group information
  • AI speech style settings
  • Community posts and comments
  • All service usage records

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I re-register with the same account after deletion?

Yes, you can. However, previous data will not be recovered and you will be registered as a new member.

Q. Can I recover data after deletion?

No, deleted data cannot be recovered under any circumstances.

Q. How long does deletion take?

Deletion through the app is immediate. Email requests are processed within 3 business days.
