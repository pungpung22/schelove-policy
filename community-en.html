Community Guidelines

Schelove - A Safe and Healthy Fan Community

Welcome

Schelove is a space where K-pop fans connect and share information together. Please follow these guidelines to ensure all users have a safe and enjoyable experience.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Schelove applies a zero tolerance policy for the following behaviors. Violations will result in immediate permanent account suspension:

  • Profanity and Vulgar Language - No form of profanity is tolerated
  • Hate Speech - Discriminatory remarks about race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, etc.
  • Harassment and Bullying - Persistent attacks targeting specific users or idols
  • Sexual Content - Pornography or sexually suggestive content
  • Violent Content - Content that promotes or threatens violence
  • Privacy Violations - Unauthorized disclosure of others' personal information
  • Spam and Fraud - Posts for advertising, phishing, or scam purposes
  • Copyright Infringement - Posting others' copyrighted work without permission

Recommended Behavior

  • Respect and be considerate of other fans
  • Create constructive conversations and a positive atmosphere
  • Share only accurate information and avoid spreading unverified rumors
  • Respect idols' privacy
  • Maintain mutual respect with other fandoms

Content Monitoring

Schelove implements the following measures for a safe community environment:

  • Automatic Filtering: Profanity and inappropriate expressions are automatically detected and blocked
  • User Reports: Report functionality is available for all posts and comments
  • Prompt Review: Reported content is reviewed within 24 hours
  • Transparent Action: Violations result in warnings, content removal, or account suspension

How to Report

Found inappropriate content? Please report using the following methods:

  • In-App Report: Select 'Report' from the more options (⋮) menu on posts/comments
  • Block User: Use the 'Block' function on user profiles
  • Email Report: For urgent matters, report directly to the contact below

Reports and Inquiries

pung0805@gmail.com

Disciplinary Actions

The following sanctions apply for guideline violations:

  • 1st Violation (Minor): Warning and content removal
  • 2nd Violation: 7-day activity suspension
  • 3rd Violation: 30-day activity suspension
  • Severe Violation: Immediate permanent suspension (Zero Tolerance Policy applies)

Note: Steps may be skipped depending on the severity of the violation. Violations under the Zero Tolerance Policy result in immediate permanent suspension, even for first-time offenders.

Appeals

If you wish to appeal a disciplinary action:

  • Send an appeal email to pung0805@gmail.com
  • Include your account information and detailed reasons for the appeal
  • We will respond within 7 days after review
